Purna Swaraj Declaration

On 26 January 1930, the Indian National Congress declared Purna Swaraj (Complete Independence) as its goal at the Lahore Session, rejecting dominion status and calling for total freedom from British rule.

Why was it important?

The declaration marked a shift from seeking dominion status to demanding full independence, inspiring mass movements and the celebration of 26 January as Independence Day until 1947.
