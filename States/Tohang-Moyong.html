Tohang Moyong

Tohang Moyong was one of the few tribal leaders from Arunachal Pradesh who strongly resisted British colonial encroachment and defended tribal sovereignty during the pre-independence era.

Biography

Tohang Moyong emerged as a strong local leader during the early 20th century in the Siang Valley. His resistance came during a time when the British were attempting to survey and integrate tribal regions without consent. Moyong organized his people to resist such moves and asserted autonomy through both strategic negotiations and confrontations.

Though not widely documented, oral traditions and local archives honor him as a protector of cultural and territorial identity in Arunachal Pradesh.

Legacy

Tohang Moyong is considered one of the foundational figures in the assertion of Arunachal tribal identity. His memory is preserved in tribal folklore and community honors, inspiring pride in cultural resistance and autonomy.
