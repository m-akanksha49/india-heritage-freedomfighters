Jai Narayan Vyas

A visionary leader and prominent freedom fighter from Rajasthan who played a significant role in the political and social development of the region.

Positions Held

Chief Minister of Rajasthan, Freedom Fighter

Biography

Jai Narayan Vyas was an Indian politician, freedom fighter, and social reformer who contributed significantly to India's struggle for independence, especially in the princely state of Jodhpur. He was actively involved in nationalist activities and founded the Jodhpur Praja Mandal to promote democratic reforms in the state.

Post-independence, he became one of the earliest Chief Ministers of Rajasthan and worked towards modernization, education, and infrastructure development in the region.

Freedom Struggle Timeline

1934

Formed the Jodhpur Praja Mandal to demand democratic reforms in princely states.

1942

Participated in the Quit India Movement; imprisoned for nationalist activities.

1947

Played a vital role in the accession of Jodhpur to the Indian Union.

1951

Became the Chief Minister of Rajasthan and implemented various reform policies.

Legacy

Public Servant

Historian
